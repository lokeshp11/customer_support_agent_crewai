Pulse Telecom Privacy & General Policies

1. Privacy Policy
   - We collect user data strictly for service provision and billing.
   - We do NOT sell personal data to third parties.
   - Data Retention: Billing records are kept for 7 years as per law. Usage logs are retained for 6 months.

2. Service Level Agreement (SLA)
   - Residential Uptime Guarantee: 99.5%
   - Business Uptime Guarantee: 99.9%
   - If uptime falls below guarantee, pro-rated credits are applied upon request.

3. Fair Use Policy (FUP)
   - "Unlimited" plans purely for residential use.
   - Continuous 24/7 heavy automated downloading may trigger a review.
   - Mobile hotspot usage is capped at speed after allowance is used (e.g., 15GB).

4. Refunds & Cancellations
   - 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee for new Broadband installations.
   - Cancellations must be made 24 hours before the next billing cycle to avoid charge.
   - Pro-rated refunds are issued only if service was non-functional.
