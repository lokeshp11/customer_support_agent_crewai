Pulse Telecom Technical Support Guide (Detailed)

1. Broadband / Fiber Internet
   - Slow Speed Issues:
     - Check valid speeds at speedtest.pulse.telecom.
     - Restart your Pulse Gateway (Router) by unplugging power for 30 seconds.
     - Move closer to the router to rule out Wi-Fi range issues.
     - Connect via Ethernet cable to verify if it's a Wi-Fi or Line issue.

   - No Internet / Red Light on Router:
     - Check for local outages on the Pulse Mobile App.
     - Ensure fiber cables are securely plugged in (do not bend fiber cables sharply).
     - If the "Optical" or "PON" light is red, a technician visit is required.

   - Wi-Fi Password Reset:
     - Log in to 192.168.1.1 (Default User/Pass: admin/password).
     - Navigate to Wireless Settings -> Security.

2. Mobile 5G/4G LTE
   - No Signal:
     - Toggle Airplane Mode on/off.
     - Restart your device.
     - Ensure "Mobile Data" and "Roaming" are enabled if traveling.
     - Update Carrier Settings (Settings -> General -> About).

   - Call Drops:
     - Enable Wi-Fi Calling as a backup in low-coverage areas.
     - Remove obstruction/cases that might block antennas.

3. Equipment Return
   - Upon cancellation, all leased equipment (Modem, Router, TV Box) must be returned within 14 days.
   - Failure to return results in a non-return fee:
     - Modem: $150
     - TV Box: $100
     - Wi-Fi Extender: $50
