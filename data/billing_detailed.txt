Pulse Telecom Billing Policies (Detailed)

1. Billing Cycle
   - All postpaid plans (Broadband and Mobile) operate on a monthly billing cycle.
   - The billing cycle starts on the day your service is activated.
   - Example: If activated on the 15th, your cycle runs from the 15th to the 14th of the next month.
   - Bills are generated 3 days after the cycle ends.

2. Payment Methods
   - Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
   - Direct Debit / ACH
   - Online Portal (mypulse.telecom)
   - Mobile App
   - Check (Processed within 5 business days)

3. Late Fees & Dues
   - Payment is due 15 days after bill generation.
   - A late fee of $10 or 5% of the outstanding amount (whichever is higher) applies if not paid by the due date.
   - Services may be suspended if dues remain unpaid for 45 days.
   - Reconnection fee after suspension is $35.

4. Taxes & Surcharges
   - Federal Universal Service Fund (FUSF) fees apply.
   - State and local sales taxes are calculated based on your service address.
   - E911 fees are mandatory for all mobile subscribers.

5. Disputes
   - If you believe there is an error in your bill, you must report it within 60 days of the bill date.
   - Contact Billing Support at billing@pulse.telecom or ask PulseAI.
